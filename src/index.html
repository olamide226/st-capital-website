Founded in August 2005, St. Stephen's Capital is an enterprise consulting firm comprising of dynamic business professionals providing outstanding Business Development services for commercial organisations.

Our Vision:

Be the Best

Our Mission:

As there is dawning realisation that national governments cannot always meet the needs of the Society, there is also growing consensus among individuals and groups to create Opportunities for Growth.

Our mission is to Empower the Entrepreneurial Class within the private sector of the Economy to develop and sustain Profitable Enterprises.

Our Strategy:

We aim to achieve these goals by ensuring we involve People at all levels in the design, installation and implementation of systems and structures with the flexibility to Reflect the ever-changing nature of our Society; sufficiently Robust to meet the Environmental Demand of our time; and Futuristic enough to successfully confront the Challenges of Tomorrow.
