Payment FAQs

Commonly asked questions about our payment process

Please include your invoice number or client ID as the payment reference. This helps us match your payment to your account efficiently.

Payments are typically processed within 1-2 business days. International transfers may take 3-5 business days depending on your bank and location.

Once your payment has been processed, we will send a confirmation email to the address associated with your account. You can also contact our finance team for confirmation.

St. Stephen's Capital does not charge additional fees for international payments. However, your bank may charge fees for international transfers. Please check with your bank for details on any potential fees.
