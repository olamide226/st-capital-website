About Us

Our Approach

We are Pioneers in everything we do and our approach to Business Development is unique. We thrive by being different from the pack.

Our Associates are recruited from within the Societies where we operate thereby utilizing local knowledge; understanding and respecting cultural and regional diversities that exist; and contributing to the uplift of the Communities.

We do not generate endless and voluminous reports full of historical data and models which are time-consuming and usually meaningless to the Executives to implement. In contrast to the norms, we concentrate on the specifics. We are, therefore, able to deliver outstanding Performance and produce Measurable Results by employing innovative methods and approach.

At the heart of St. Stephen's Capital is a Pioneer Chartered Marketer with a wealth of business development and entrepreneurial experience with world-class organisations spanning almost three decades.

Our speciality is in the design and execution of goal-driven business development strategies and we pride ourselves in our round-the-clock involvement in the implementation of our recommendations and ensuring we deliver Measurable Results to our Partners and Stakeholders regardless of size.
